# Baseerah AI

AI-powered smart city platform designed to monitor, analyze, and visualize urban data in real time using intelligent digital twin technologies.

---

## Overview

Baseerah AI is an advanced smart city platform focused on improving urban management through artificial intelligence, real-time analytics, and interactive monitoring systems.

The platform aims to provide a unified dashboard for monitoring:

- Traffic and congestion
- Electricity consumption
- Water usage
- Smart alerts and incidents
- Urban analytics
- AI-based predictions
- Smart city insights

---

## Vision

Baseerah AI aligns with modern smart city initiatives and digital transformation goals by delivering intelligent urban monitoring solutions powered by AI technologies.

---

## Core Features

### Real-Time Monitoring
Live monitoring dashboards for city infrastructure and urban systems.

### AI Predictions
Predictive analytics for traffic, energy usage, and urban patterns.

### Smart Alerts
Automated intelligent notifications for abnormal activities and incidents.

### Interactive Dashboard
Modern responsive dashboard with data visualization and analytics.

### Mobile Integration
Cross-platform mobile application connected to the same backend infrastructure.
